About My Cats

Huahua and Zuozuo are a huge part of my life. They are sisters and came to hour home when they were only 2 months old.

They helped me get over the most difficult period of time. I am so lucky and grateful to have them in my life.

Last summer, Zuozuo left us due to a sudden heart attack. It was a disaster, but meanwhile she taught me how selfless love can be, and how precious those memories are.

Now Huahua is 6 years old. I wish she can always be happy and healthy.
